The City of Hebron

The embroidery of Hebron is one of the most prominent styles of Palestinian embroidery, known for its boldness, strong colors, and rich heritage symbols.

Features of Hebron Embroidery

① Colors

  • Vibrant red is the primary color.
  • Extensive use of black as a background or main color.
  • Touches of green, orange, and yellow to add vitality.

② Stitches

The most common stitch is the Felahi (Cross) stitch, executed densely, giving the embroidery strength and a prominent presence.

③ Common Symbols

  • Hebron Veins: Long, parallel lines resembling branches.
  • Moon Flower: A symbol of femininity and beauty.
  • Cypress Tree: Symbolizes life and continuity.
  • Wheat Ears: Reflect the agricultural character of the region.
  • Eight-Pointed Star: A symbol of protection and radiance.
