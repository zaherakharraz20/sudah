The City of Safad

Safad is distinguished by embroidery that blends simplicity with geometric patterns, reflecting the surrounding nature and local traditions.

Features of Safad Embroidery

① Colors

  • Green — symbol of nature and agriculture.
  • Red — a strong traditional color.
  • Blue — inspired by the sky and nearby sea.
  • Black — to outline patterns and highlight contrast.

② Stitches

  • Filé (Cross) Stitch for large patterns.
  • Chain Stitch for fabric borders and edges.
  • Dot Stitch for small details and delicate decorations.

③ Common Symbols

  • Wildflowers: Symbolize beauty and natural life.
  • Small Birds: Symbolize freedom and joy.
  • Repeated Geometric Shapes: Inspired by traditional architecture and old houses.
