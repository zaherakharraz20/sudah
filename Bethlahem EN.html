The City of Bethlehem

Bethlehem is distinguished by its richly detailed embroidery, often used for formal and ceremonial clothing, with touches of gold in some designs.

Features of Bethlehem Embroidery

① Colors

  • Red — symbol of authenticity and strength.
  • Black — to outline patterns and highlight contrast.
  • Blue — to add depth to decorations.
  • Gold — for luxury and ceremonial embellishments.

② Stitches

  • Filé (Cross) Stitch for large and repetitive patterns.
  • Dot Stitch — for fine details and small decorations.
  • Pull Stitch — used in summer and lightweight clothing.
  • Chain Stitch for fabric borders and edges.

③ Common Symbols

  • Large Roses and Flowers: symbolize beauty and life.
  • Repeated Geometric Patterns: inspired by traditional architecture and old houses.
  • Religious Elements: to add a ceremonial character.
